Project Details:

While Steam currently implements its own sentiment analysis to gauge user opinions, my aim is to construct a more intricate model that delves deeper into the nuances of user feedback. By leveraging advanced data science techniques and a comprehensive understanding of natural language processing, the goal is to create a model that not only parallels but potentially surpasses the accuracy of existing systems. This endeavor is rooted in the belief that there's always room for improvement and innovation, especially in the dynamic and ever-evolving realm of sentiment analysis. Plus, we REALLY need to know what people think about Spore in 2024.
